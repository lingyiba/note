- #### Protect spine #idea
    - When looking up
        - Pull the chin up
    - When pressing the neck
        - Rotate to that hand
    - Surrounding the shoulder joint
        - Shake hands, directly in front of shoulder joint
        - Pull elbows up to the top
        - Pull apart on both sides
- ***Notes***
    - Don't bow the head
