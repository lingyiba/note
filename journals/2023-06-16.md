- #### Use mathematical formulas in Markdown
    - `$\frac{2+i}{i}$` # $\frac{2+i}{i}$
    - `$\\{x|x \ge 2\\}=U-(M \cup N)$` # $\\{x|x \ge 2\\}=U-(M \cup N)$
    - `$\bar{z}=$` # $\bar{z}=$
- ***Notes***
    - `^{}` # Superscript
    - `\frac{}{}` # Fraction
        - `\over` # Another way (Numberator divided by denomiator)
    - `_{}` # Subscript
    - `\cup` # Union
    - `\ge` # Greater than or equal to
