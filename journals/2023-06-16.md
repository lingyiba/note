- #### Use mathematical formulas in Markdown
    - `${{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}} \over {2a}}$`
- ***Notes***
    - `\pm` # Plus or minus
    - `\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}` # Square root
    - `^` # Superscript
    - `\over` # Fraction (Numerator divided by denominator)
