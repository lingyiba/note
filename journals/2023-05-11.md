- #### Use "systemctl"
    - `doas systemctl enable cronie`
        - `doas /etc/init.d/cronie start`
        - Every time at boot time
    - `doas systemctl start cronie`
        - `doas rc-update add cronie default`
        - Once now
- ***Notes***
    - `cronie` # Replace it with your service
