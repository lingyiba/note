- #### Make .nix-profile/bin/ effective in Dmenu
    - `vi ~/.bash_profile`
      ```
      if [[ -d "$HOME/.nix-profile/bin/" ]]; then
          export PATH="$PATH:$HOME/.nix-profile/bin/"
      fi
      ```
    - `reboot`
    - `rm ~/.cache/dmenu_run` # Clear cache
