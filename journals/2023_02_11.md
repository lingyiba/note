    - The relative tools (e.g. ssh, sftp, scp) are parts of the OpenSSH.

    - `gpasswd -a yaoniplan vboxusers`
- ***Notes***
    - `-a` # Add
    - `yaoniplan` # Replace it with your user name
    - `vboxusers` # Repalce it with your group name
