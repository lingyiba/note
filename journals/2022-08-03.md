- #### Move spam to inbox automatically in Gmail
    - Click "Create a new filter" in "Settings"
    - Type "is:spam" in the "Has the words"
    - Click "Create filter" and "OK"
    - Click "Never send it to Spam" checkbox
    - Click "Create filter"
- ***Notes***
    - Because to unify all mail.
