- #### Stop a process in Linux
    - `kill -STOP 508`
- ***Notes***
    - `508` # Replace it with your process ID
        - `ps aux | grep mpv`
    - `kill -CONT 508` # Continue

- #### Use "awk"
    - `kill -STOP $(ps aux | grep -v grep | grep aola | awk '{print $2}')`
- ***Notes***
    - `aola` # Replace it with your desired command
    - `$2` # Second column
