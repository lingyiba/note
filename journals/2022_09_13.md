- [[Python]] `1 > 2 and not 1 > 3 or 2 > 3` priority (优先)
  * 等价于 `((1 > 2) and (not (1 > 3))) or (2 > 3)`
  * `(False and (not False)) or False` # 算术运算符 > 逻辑运算符
  * `(False and True) or False` # 逻辑运算符 `not`
  * `False or False` # 逻辑运算符 `and`
  * `False` # 逻辑运算符 `or`
  * ***总结***: 算术运算符 > 逻辑运算符(not > and > or)
